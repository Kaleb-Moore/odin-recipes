Chicken Nachos

Description

Nachos... what else do you need. Good for both parties with friends, or crying and eating alone. Either way they are delicious!


ingredients

  • Nacho Chips
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Feel free to use any spices or toppings you want.

Directions

  1. Set Oven to 400F and cover a baking sheet with tin foil.
  2. Cook your chicken with any spices you want
  3. Cover baking tray with Nacho Chips
  4. Add shredded cheese and any toppings you want
  5. Bake in the oven for 5 - 10 mins
  6. ENJOY!
